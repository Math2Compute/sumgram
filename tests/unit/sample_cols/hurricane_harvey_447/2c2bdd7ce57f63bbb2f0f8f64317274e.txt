The city and county have authorized a disaster relief account at gofundme.com/victoria-tx-disaster-relief-fund and has raised $5,520.
Volunteers are urgently needed to help residents clean out homes damaged by Hurricane Harvey.
About 700 work orders are being worked on by volunteers with the nonprofit Samaritan's Purse.
People interested in volunteering should call the hotline to sign up for a safety briefing and be referred to a volunteer coordinator.
Voluntary Organizations Active in Disaster has taken over the Victoria Responds Call Center hotline to help connect affected people with resources.
The new phone number is 361-433-2785 with the old number being forwarded and runs from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Affected people and families should pre-register with both FEMA at 1-800-621-3362 and with the American Red Cross at 1-866-526-8300.
