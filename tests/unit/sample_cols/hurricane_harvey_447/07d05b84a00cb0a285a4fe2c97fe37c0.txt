Tropical Storm Harvey forecast to become Cat 3 hurricane
Posted:
Updated:
Tropical Storm Harvey is strengthening in the Gulf and is now forecast to become a major Category 3 Hurricane for the Coastal Bend.
All preparations should be rapidly taking place for the arrival of this major hurricane as of 10 a.m. 
By tonight the storm will strengthening to a Cat 1 and will be a Cat 2 by Friday morning.
As the storm moves ashore all estimates have been increased to major levels.
Winds up to 115 mph are now expected.  Rainfall of 10 - 20 inches expected. Storm Surge for 6 - 10 feet expected.
