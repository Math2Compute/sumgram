Rockport Officials: 1 dead, 14 injured, 9 missing following Harvey
Posted:
Photo of damaged building in Rockport. Not where fatality or injuries occurred.
ROCKPORT -
The community of Rockport took in a major toll of damage with the brunt of Hurricane Harvey. 
The entire community is without power, water, electricity and cell phone service. 
According to officials, the has reportedly been one fatality due to a house fire overnight.
Officials say the body was not discovered until Saturday morning and the identity of the person has not been released. 
There were also 14 people who were reportedly injured, but we're told all injuries were minor. 
Nine people have been unaccounted for, according to officials. 
If you did evacuate for Hurricane Harvey, city officials are strongly encouraging resident not to return at this time.
The city will post information on their website as to when the city will be able to allow people back.
