Depression becomes Tropical Storm Harvey
By News Release
Aug. 23, 2017 at 11:24 p.m.
Updated Aug. 23, 2017 at 11:24 p.m.
Tropical Depression Harvey is now Tropical Storm Harvey, as of 11 p.m. Wednesday.
The National Hurricane Center's 11 p.m. update upgraded the storm, which remains on the same path, a northwest jog at 2 mph.
The latest forecast calls for Tropical Storm Harvey to upgrade to a hurricane before making landfall somewhere along the Texas Coast.
The forecast expects the storm to make landfall late Friday night into early Saturday morning.
